Before You Start

Your Responsibilities

  • Warmly greet everyone
  • Ask for the QR code on arrival
  • No QR code? Look up by name
  • Verify ID for adult competitors
  • Confirm bracket, mat, and competition time
  • Apply competitor wristbands
  • Issue Parent Spectator Bands — Youth/Juvenile
  • Notify supervisor of any issues
