Why This Station Matters

Recording Weight Accurately Drives the System Forward

  • Accuracy first. The result you record drives the tournament. A wrong or missing result can ruin somebody's day.
  • Record, then stamp. The system entry — not the stamp — is what clears a competitor. Always record the result before you stamp.
  • Keep it moving. Weigh-in is the gateway to the mats. A smooth, fast line keeps the whole event on schedule.

The One Rule That Cannot Be Broken

Record the Result FIRST

The instant a competitor is on the scale

Click "Made Weight" or "Did Not Make Weight" — before the stamp, before anything else.

1. On the scale → 2. Click the result → 3. Then stamp

A stamp isn't what clears a competitor — the recorded result is.
